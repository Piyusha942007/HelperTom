SECTION 1: RETURNS & REFUND POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Health Supplements and Nutraceuticals (Vitamins, Minerals, Herbal Supplements): Non-returnable once the safety seal is broken. Unopened products returnable within 7 days.
- Ayurvedic, Homeopathic, and Traditional Medicine Products: Non-returnable once seal is broken. Unopened products returnable within 7 days.
- Prescription Medications: Non-returnable under any circumstances (Indian pharmacy regulations).
- OTC (Over-the-Counter) Medicines: Non-returnable once the seal/packaging is opened.
- Health Devices (BP Monitors, Glucometers, Thermometers, Oximeters, Nebulizers): 7 days (Replacement Only for defects).
- Medical Equipment (Wheelchairs, Hospital Beds, CPAP Machines): 10 days (Replacement Only for defects).
- Personal Wellness Devices (Massagers, Heating Pads, Posture Correctors): 7 days.
- Orthopedic Supports (Braces, Splints, Compression Wear): Non-returnable once worn or packaging opened (hygiene).
- Sexual Wellness Products: Non-returnable once packaging is opened (hygiene and safety).
- First Aid Supplies: Non-returnable once packaging is opened (sterility concerns).
- Aromatherapy and Essential Oils: 7 days if unopened and seal intact.
- Yoga and Meditation Accessories: 7 days (follows Sports & Fitness policy).

1.2 Condition for Returns
- Supplements and Consumables: Must be completely unopened with the original factory seal, safety band, or shrink wrap intact. Any sign of tampering, opening, or seal breakage leads to rejection.
- Health Devices: Must be in original packaging with all accessories (cuffs, probes, batteries, manuals). Devices that have been used on the body must be thoroughly cleaned before return (BP cuff, thermometer probe, etc.).
- Medical Equipment: Must be in original packaging, unassembled (if self-assembled, must be disassembled), with all parts and documentation.
- Wellness Devices: Must show no signs of extensive use. Personal massagers and body-contact devices — hygiene restrictions apply.
- Orthopedic Supports: Must be in sealed original packaging. Once packaging is opened and the support is tried on the body, it becomes non-returnable.

1.3 Non-Returnable Health Products (Strict List)
The following are strictly non-returnable once the packaging seal is broken, for health, hygiene, and safety reasons:
- All opened supplements, vitamins, nutraceuticals, protein powders, and health drinks
- All opened prescription and OTC medications
- All opened Ayurvedic, Homeopathic, Unani, and Siddha medicines
- Needles, syringes, lancets, and any sharps (even if unopened, some are non-returnable per regulations)
- Opened first aid supplies (bandages, gauze, tapes, antiseptic wipes)
- Opened orthopedic supports, braces, splints, and compression stockings
- Opened sexual wellness products (all)
- Opened pregnancy tests, ovulation kits, and diagnostic test kits
- Opened contact lenses and eye care products
- Opened hearing aids and hearing aid batteries
- Opened essential oils and aromatherapy products
- Opened nebulizer masks, tubing, and accessories (consumable/hygiene)
- Opened CPAP masks, tubing, and humidifier chambers (consumable/hygiene)

1.4 Defective Product Policy
- Defects covered:
  - Health devices that don't power on or show error codes out of the box
  - Inaccurate readings on BP monitors, glucometers, thermometers (compared with calibrated reference devices)
  - Supplements with broken seals or tampered packaging upon delivery
  - Expired products at the time of delivery
  - Wrong product: wrong variant, dosage, size, or entirely different product delivered
  - Damaged packaging: crushed boxes, punctured bottles, leaking liquids
  - Medical equipment with mechanical or electrical failure
  - Massagers that don't function or have exposed wiring
  - Heating pads that don't heat up or have hot spots (safety hazard)

- Defects NOT covered:
  - "I didn't feel any benefit" or "It didn't work for me" for supplements (efficacy is individual)
  - Side effects from supplements or medications (consult your doctor before use; stop and consult if side effects occur)
  - Taste or texture preference for supplements
  - Blood pressure/pulse oximeter readings that differ from a different brand's device (all devices have accuracy tolerances)
  - Allergic reactions (patch test before use; discontinue if reaction occurs)
  - Devices damaged by dropping, liquid exposure, or improper use

1.5 Health Device Replacement Process
- Step 1: Report the defect within 7 days with detailed description and photos/video of the malfunction.
- Step 2: For devices, a technical support call may be scheduled to troubleshoot and confirm the defect.
- Step 3: Return pickup is arranged.
- Step 4: Device undergoes technical inspection (3-5 business days).
- Step 5: If confirmed defective, a replacement unit is shipped. If out of stock, a refund is issued.
- Step 6: If the device is found to be functioning correctly (no defect), it is returned to the customer with an inspection report. The customer may be charged for return shipping.

1.6 Expiry and Shelf Life
- All health supplements, medicines, and consumable wellness products must have a minimum shelf life of 3 months from the delivery date, unless explicitly sold as "Short Expiry Clearance" with a clear disclosure.
- Expired products at delivery are a critical violation. Report within 48 hours with a photo of the expiry date. Full refund plus goodwill credit (INR 200-500) will be provided. The batch will be immediately quarantined.

1.7 Refund Timelines
- Unopened supplement/consumable returns: 5-7 business days after quality check.
- Defective health devices (confirmed): Replacement in 5-7 business days, or refund in 7-10 business days if replacement unavailable.

SECTION 5: HEALTH & WELLNESS FAQ (30 QUESTIONS)
----------------------------------------------

Q1: I bought vitamin D supplements, but after opening the bottle, the pills have a strange odor. Is this normal?
A: A strange or off-putting odor from supplements is not normal and may indicate spoilage or contamination. Do not consume them. Photograph the bottle with the batch number and report within 48 hours (even if the outer seal was broken, if the product is clearly defective). A replacement or refund will be provided.

Q2: My BP monitor gives readings that are consistently 10-15 points different from my doctor's manual reading. Is the device defective?
A: Home BP monitors have an accuracy tolerance (typically ±3 mmHg for professional validation). A consistent 10-15 point difference may indicate a calibration issue or improper cuff size/placement. First, ensure the cuff fits your arm circumference (wrong size causes inaccurate readings). Test it against another validated device. If confirmed inaccurate, report within 7 days for replacement.

Q3: Can I return an opened box of Ayurvedic chyawanprash because I don't like the taste?
A: No. Opened consumable health products are non-returnable for taste preference. The taste is a characteristic of the ingredients. We recommend buying the smallest size first to test palatability if you're new to a product.

Q4: I ordered a whey protein supplement. The safety seal was broken when I opened the outer lid. What should I do?
A: Do not consume the product. Photograph the broken seal immediately upon discovery. Report within 48 hours. A full replacement or refund will be provided. Never consume supplements with compromised seals — they could be contaminated or tampered with.

Q5: My digital thermometer shows "ERR" every time I try to use it. New batteries didn't help. Is it defective?
A: Yes, a persistent error message despite new batteries and proper placement indicates a defect. Report within 7 days with a video showing the error. A replacement thermometer will be shipped.

Q6: Can I return a posture corrector that I wore for a day and found uncomfortable?
A: No. Once an orthopedic support or posture corrector has been worn on the body, it is non-returnable for hygiene reasons. Comfort levels vary, and trying it on over clothing may not give the same feel. Research and read reviews before purchasing.

Q7: I received a bottle of fish oil capsules that expires next month. The product page didn't mention short expiry. Is this acceptable?
A: No. Supplements should have a minimum of 3 months to expiry unless marked as "Short Expiry Clearance." Report within 7 days with a photo of the expiry date. A replacement with a longer shelf life or a full refund will be provided.

Q8: My glucometer test strips are giving error messages with the matching meter. What could be wrong?
A: First, check that the strips are not expired (expired strips cause errors), the code chip (if applicable) is inserted correctly, and the strips are from the correct batch. Test with the control solution provided. If errors persist across multiple strips from the same vial, report within 7 days. A replacement vial of strips will be provided. Defective strips are a known quality issue.

Q9: Can I return a heating pad that gets too hot and burns my skin even on the lowest setting?
A: Yes. Overheating is a serious safety defect. Stop using immediately. Report within 7 days with a description and photos of any burn. A replacement or refund will be provided. Safety concerns like this are prioritized.

Q10: I bought a nebulizer, but the mist output is very weak, and treatment takes too long. Is this normal?
A: Nebulizer mist output rate is specified (ml/min). If the output is significantly lower than specified, check that the medication cup is properly assembled, the filter is clean, and the tubing is not kinked. If the issue persists, report within 7 days with a video. A replacement nebulizer will be shipped.

Q11: Can I return an unopened bottle of multivitamins if I decided to go with a different brand?
A: Yes. An unopened supplement bottle with the factory seal and safety band completely intact can be returned within 7 days. Change of mind is accepted for unopened consumables.

Q12: My new CPAP machine makes a whistling noise that keeps me awake. Is this a defect?
A: Some CPAP noise is normal. Whistling often indicates a leak in the mask seal, tubing connection, or humidifier chamber seal. Check all connections and ensure the water chamber is properly seated. If the noise persists after troubleshooting, report within 10 days. A technician call or replacement will be arranged.

Q13: I bought a pregnancy test kit, and the result window showed no lines at all — not even the control line. Is the test defective?
A: A completely blank result window (no control line) indicates an invalid test, usually due to insufficient urine or a defective test strip. This is a manufacturing defect. Report within 7 days with a photo of the invalid test. A replacement kit will be provided.

Q14: Can I return compression socks if I wore them once and they didn't help with my swelling?
A: No. Once worn, compression wear is non-returnable for hygiene reasons. Compression level (mmHg) and sizing are critical for efficacy. Ensure you selected the correct compression level prescribed/recommended and the right size based on measurements. Efficacy also depends on the underlying medical condition.

Q15: My essential oil diffuser stopped producing mist after two uses. I cleaned it as instructed. What now?
A: First, ensure the water level is correct and the water sensor isn't blocked. Use a cotton swab with rubbing alcohol to clean the ultrasonic plate. If it still doesn't work, report within 7 days with a video. A replacement diffuser will be shipped. Essential oil quality can affect diffuser function; use 100% pure oils.

Q16: I ordered a specific strength of homeopathic medicine (30C), but received 200C. What should I do?
A: Potency is critical in homeopathy. Report within 7 days with a photo of the incorrect potency. This is a "Wrong Product" delivery. The correct potency will be shipped. Do not consume the wrong potency unless advised by your homeopath.

Q17: My personal massager makes a grinding noise and vibrates erratically. Can I return it?
A: Grinding noise and erratic vibration indicate a mechanical defect. Report within 7 days with a video. A replacement will be provided. Do not continue using a malfunctioning massager as it can cause injury.

Q18: Can I return a box of band-aids if the packaging is slightly crushed but the band-aids inside look fine?
A: For sterile medical supplies, packaging integrity is critical. Even if the band-aids look fine, crushed packaging may have compromised sterility. Report within 48 hours with a photo. A replacement box will be shipped. Do not use potentially non-sterile supplies on open wounds.

Q19: I bought a weight loss supplement. Not only did I not lose weight, I felt jittery and unwell. Can I return it?
A: Stop taking the supplement immediately and consult a doctor. Report the adverse reaction to HelperTom. While opened supplements are generally non-returnable for efficacy, adverse reactions are taken seriously. Our pharmacovigilance team will investigate, and a refund may be issued. The product's safety profile will be reviewed.

Q20: My oximeter shows my SpO2 as 85% when I'm feeling perfectly fine. Is it broken?
A: An 85% reading in a healthy, asymptomatic person likely indicates a faulty device or improper use. Ensure your hand is warm, still, and the oximeter is placed correctly (no nail polish). Test on multiple healthy individuals. If consistently showing critically low readings, report within 7 days. A replacement will be shipped.

Q21: I ordered a memory foam pillow for neck pain. After a week, my neck pain is worse. Can I return the pillow?
A: Pillows, once used, are non-returnable for hygiene reasons (contact with face, hair, skin oils). Neck pain adjustment periods are common with new pillows (7-14 days). If the pillow was marketed with a "comfort guarantee" or trial period (stated on product page), you may return it under that specific program.

Q22: Can I return an opened pack of diabetic-friendly sugar substitute if it has a bitter aftertaste?
A: No. Opened consumables are non-returnable for taste preference. Many sugar substitutes have characteristic aftertastes (stevia bitterness, cooling effect of erythritol). Research the sweetener type before purchasing.

Q23: My air purifier's filter replacement light came on after only 2 weeks of use. Is this normal?
A: Filter life depends on air quality and usage hours. In very polluted areas, filters can saturate quickly. However, a filter indicator after only 2 weeks of continuous use is unusually fast. Check if the pre-filter needs cleaning. If the issue persists, report within 7 days. The filter or sensor may be defective.

Q24: I received a bottle of herbal capsules with a different color powder inside than my previous bottle. The batch numbers are different. Is this a quality issue?
A: Natural herbal powders can vary in color between harvests and batches due to growing conditions, season, and processing. Different batch numbers will have slight variations. This is normal for natural products. If the powder has an off-smell, mold, or radically different appearance, report within 7 days.

Q25: Can I return a wheelchair if my family member finds it uncomfortable for long sitting periods?
A: Wheelchairs are medical equipment with a 10-day Replacement Only policy for defects. Discomfort is not a defect. Proper fit (seat width, depth, back height) is critical and should be assessed before purchase. We recommend consulting an occupational therapist for wheelchair fitting.

Q26: My digital weighing scale shows a different weight every time I step on it, even a few seconds apart. Is it defective?
A: Inconsistent readings can be caused by uneven flooring, low battery, or stepping on/off too quickly. Place the scale on a hard, flat surface. Replace the batteries. Step on, let it calibrate to zero, then weigh. If readings still fluctuate wildly (more than 0.5 kg variation within seconds), report within 7 days. A replacement will be provided.

Q27: I bought iron supplement gummies. They melted into a single blob during delivery in summer. Can I get a refund?
A: Yes. Heat damage during transit is a quality issue. Photograph the melted product and report within 24 hours. A refund or replacement will be provided. We recommend ordering heat-sensitive supplements during cooler months or opting for express shipping in summer.

Q28: Can I return an unopened contact lens solution bottle if I switched to daily disposables?
A: Yes, if the bottle is completely unopened with the factory seal intact, within 7 days. Once opened, contact lens solutions are non-returnable due to sterility requirements.

Q29: My hearing aid produces a constant high-pitched feedback whistle even when properly inserted. Is this defective?
A: Feedback (whistling) can occur due to earwax blockage, improper fit, or volume being too high. Clean the hearing aid, check the ear dome size, and lower the volume. If feedback persists across different environments and volumes, report within 7 days. The hearing aid may have a defective feedback cancellation system. A replacement will be provided.

Q30: I ordered a knee brace in size Large based on the size chart, but it's too tight. Can I exchange for XL?
A: No. Once an orthopedic brace's packaging is opened and the brace is tried on, it is non-returnable for hygiene reasons. This is why it's critical to measure carefully (circumference at the joint, not just height/weight) before ordering. Consult the size guide with actual body measurements.
