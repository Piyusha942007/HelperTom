----------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: RETURNS & EXCHANGE POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Customers may initiate a return or exchange within 7 days from the date of delivery for all Clothing and Fashion items.
- For items marked as "No Returns" or "Non-Returnable" (e.g., innerwear, lingerie, socks, shapewear), returns or exchanges are not accepted except in cases of damaged, defective, or incorrect product delivery.
- The 7-day window applies uniformly to all price ranges, brands, and discounts unless otherwise stated on the product page.

1.2 Condition for Returns
- Items must be returned unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags intact.
- The original packaging, brand boxes, and any accompanying accessories (belts, brooches, detachable elements) must be returned along with the product.
- Items that show signs of wear, deodorant stains, makeup smudges, perfume odor, or damage caused by the customer will be rejected during the quality check.

1.3 Non-Returnable Items (Category-Specific)
The following Fashion & Clothing subcategories are non-returnable for hygiene and safety reasons:
- Innerwear, lingerie, briefs, boxers, panties
- Socks and stockings
- Shapewear and bodysuits
- Swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken)
- Earrings and nose pins (for hygiene)
- Freebies, gifts, or promotional items bundled with clothing

1.4 Exchange Policy
- Exchanges are permitted only for a different size of the same product, subject to availability.
- If the desired size is unavailable, the order will be processed as a return and refund.
- Exchange requests must be placed within the same 7-day return window.
- One exchange per order line item is allowed.

1.5 Refund Timelines
- Once the returned item passes the quality check at the warehouse, the refund is initiated.
- Refund Method & Timeline:
  - To original payment method (Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI): 5-7 business days after approval.
  - To Wallet / Store Credit: Within 24 hours of approval.
  - Cash on Delivery refunds: Transferred to bank account or wallet within 5-7 business days after account details are verified.
- Return shipping charges (if any) are deducted from the refund amount unless the return is due to a seller error (defective/wrong item).

1.6 Pickup and Processing
- Free return pickups are provided for eligible pin codes. In non-serviceable areas, customers must self-ship the item via a recommended courier partner.
- Self-ship reimbursements (up to INR 100) are credited as store credit upon successful quality check.

1.7 Quality Check and Rejection
- Each returned product undergoes a quality inspection.
- Reasons for rejection include:
  - Item not in original condition
  - Tags or labels removed
  - Missing accessories or packaging
  - Signs of use, washing, or alteration
- Rejected items are returned to the customer. Return shipping for rejected items may be chargeable.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: CANCELLATION POLICY
----------------------------------------------

2.1 Cancellation Window
- Orders can be cancelled within 30 minutes of placement or before the order moves to "Packed" status, whichever is earlier.
- For prepaid orders, the refund is initiated immediately upon cancellation.
- For Cash on Delivery orders, no charges are levied for cancellation within the allowed window.

2.2 Post-Window Cancellation
- If the cancellation window has passed, the customer must wait for delivery and then initiate a return as per the return policy.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: WARRANTY AND DEFECTS
----------------------------------------------

3.1 Manufacturing Defects
- Fashion & Clothing items do not carry a standard warranty period. However, if a manufacturing defect (e.g., faulty zipper, seam tear, dye bleeding) is reported within the 7-day return window, the item qualifies for a full refund or exchange.
- Beyond 7 days, complaints of general wear and tear, color fading due to washing, or fit issues are not covered.

3.2 Damage in Transit
- If the package appears tampered with or damaged at the time of delivery, the customer is advised to refuse delivery or open the package in front of the delivery agent and document with photographs.
- Claims for transit damage must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
----------------------------------------------

4.1 Delivery Timelines
- Standard Delivery: 4-8 business days for metro cities, 7-12 business days for non-metro locations.
- Express Delivery (if available): 1-3 business days in selected pin codes, with additional charges.
- Delivery timelines are indicative and may vary during peak sale periods, festivals, or due to force majeure events.

4.2 Shipping Charges
- Free standard shipping on orders above INR 499.
- Orders below INR 499 are charged a flat shipping fee of INR 49-99 depending on the delivery location.
- Express delivery charges vary by pin code and are displayed at checkout.

SECTION 5: FASHION & CLOTHING FAQ
----------------------------------------------

Q1: Can I return a shirt if I don't like the color or fit?
A: Yes. Clothing items can be returned within 7 days for any reason, provided they are unused, unwashed, and have all original tags and packaging intact. The refund is processed after a successful quality check.

Q2: I ordered a dress but received a different size/color. What should I do?
A: If you received an incorrect item, initiate a return request within 7 days. Select the reason "Wrong item received." Our team will arrange a free return pickup and process a full refund or exchange for the correct product, subject to stock availability.

Q3: Can I exchange a top for a different size?
A: Yes, size exchanges are permitted within the 7-day return window. If your desired size is in stock, it will be shipped to you once the original item is picked up. If the size is unavailable, the amount will be refunded.

Q4: What happens if my return package gets lost in transit during the return?
A: If you used our arranged pickup service, the responsibility lies with us. We will initiate the refund once the tracking shows the package has not moved for a specified period or is confirmed lost. If you self-shipped, we will coordinate with the courier; please retain the shipping receipt.

Q5: Can I return multiple clothing items from the same order separately?
A: Yes, you can initiate returns for individual items within the same order. Each item will be inspected independently.

Q6: I wore a kurta once and washed it, but now I want to return it. Is that possible?
A: No. Washed, worn, or used items do not qualify for returns or exchanges as they fail the mandatory quality check.

Q7: What if the clothing item has a slight manufacturing defect like a loose thread?
A: Minor variations like loose threads do not qualify as manufacturing defects. However, significant defects like broken zippers, torn seams, or unfixable stains are covered. Report such issues within the 7-day return window with clear photographs.

Q8: Can I return a saree with a fall and pico that I got done externally?
A: No. Any alterations made after delivery (fall pico, hemming, custom tailoring) make the product ineligible for return or refund.

Q9: How long does it take to get my money back after returning a clothing item?
A: After the item passes the quality check (typically 2-4 business days post pickup), refunds to the original payment method take 5-7 business days. Wallet credits are instant upon approval.

Q10: Is there a limit on how many clothing items I can return across orders?
A: Our platform monitors return patterns. Excessively high return rates may trigger a warning, temporary suspension of return privileges, or account review to prevent misuse. This applies to all product categories.

Q11: Can I return a pair of jeans if the color bleeds during the first wash?
A: If the color bleed occurs on the first wash despite following the care instructions, you may raise a claim within the return window. Attach images showing the issue. Our quality team will review and approve a refund if it is a manufacturing defect. However, normal color bleeding (as warned on care labels) is not a defect.

Q12: Do you offer a try-and-buy service for fashion products?
A: Currently, we do not offer a try-and-buy service. All purchases follow the standard 7-day return policy.

Q13: Can I cancel one item from a multi-product clothing order?
A: You can cancel individual items as long as the entire order has not been packed. Once an item is packed, cancellation is not available, and you must wait to receive it and then initiate a return.

Q14: What is the return policy for fashion accessories like scarves, stoles, or belts?
A: Fashion accessories can be returned within 7 days if they are unused, with all tags and original packaging intact.

Q15: Are men's suits and blazers returnable?
A: Yes, suits and blazers fall under the standard clothing return policy (7 days). They must be returned with all tags, hangers, and garment covers included. Wrinkles from trying on at home are acceptable; stains, odors, or missing tags are not.
