SECTION 1: RETURNS & REFUND POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Beauty & Skincare products are eligible for return within 7 days from the date of delivery, subject to strict hygiene and safety conditions.
- Due to the intimate nature of these products, returns are ONLY accepted in cases of:
  - Damaged product received (leakage, breakage, spillage)
  - Defective product (unusual odor, texture, color different from description)
  - Wrong product delivered (incorrect shade, variant, size, or entirely different product)
  - Expired product delivered at the time of receipt
  - Allergic reaction (subject to medical verification)
- "Change of mind" or "I don't like the fragrance" returns are NOT accepted for any Beauty & Skincare products once the seal is broken or the product is used.

1.2 Non-Returnable Beauty Products
The following items are strictly non-returnable once delivered, unless damaged or defective:
- Lipsticks, lip glosses, lip balms, lip liners, and all lip products
- Mascaras, eyeliners, kajal, eye shadows, and all eye makeup products
- Foundations, concealers, compact powders, and all face makeup
- Nail polishes, nail art kits, and nail care products
- Perfumes, deodorants, body sprays, and fragrances (flammable material restrictions)
- Essential oils and aromatherapy products
- Face masks (sheet, clay, peel-off) and single-use treatments
- Serums, creams, lotions, and moisturizers with broken seals
- Sunscreens and sun protection products with broken seals
- Hair dyes, hair colors, and bleaching products
- Waxing kits, hair removal creams, and depilatory products
- Intimate hygiene products and personal care items
- Bath bombs, bath salts, and bath oils with broken seals
- Sample sizes, trial packs, and sachets
- Gift sets and combo packs where any single item seal is broken

1.3 Condition for Return (Eligible Products)
- Product must be unused, unopened, and in its original sealed packaging.
- The protective seal, shrink wrap, or tamper-evident sticker must be fully intact.
- All accompanying accessories (applicators, spatulas, brushes, droppers) must be returned.
- Products received as part of "Buy 1 Get 1" or combo offers must be returned in full. Partial returns of bundled items are not accepted.
- Batch numbers and expiry dates on the returned product must match the shipped record.

1.4 Damaged or Defective Product Claims
- Leakage, spillage, breakage, or crushed packaging must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with unboxing video or clear photographs.
- For damaged products, the outer packaging, inner packaging, and the damaged product itself must all be photographed and uploaded.
- A replacement of the same product is offered. If stock is unavailable, a full refund is processed.
- No pickup is required for completely damaged/leaked products in some cases; photographic evidence may suffice, and a replacement or refund is processed directly.

1.5 Allergic Reaction Claims
- If a product causes an allergic reaction (rashes, irritation, swelling, burns, breakouts), discontinue use immediately.
- Report the incident to HelperTom support within 7 days of delivery along with:
  - Photographs of the reaction on skin
  - Photograph of the product with batch number visible
  - A brief description of when the reaction started and which product was used
  - A medical certificate or doctor's note (may be requested for severe reactions)
- Our quality team will review the case. If approved, a refund or store credit will be issued. The product may or may not need to be returned depending on the severity.
- One-time courtesy refunds for allergic reactions are at the platform's discretion. Repeated claims may be flagged for review.

1.6 Expired or Near-Expiry Products
- Products delivered with an expiry date less than 3 months from the delivery date are eligible for return or replacement.
- Products already expired at the time of delivery must be reported within 48 hours for an immediate refund or replacement.
- Clearance sale items with explicit "Short Expiry" disclosures on the product page are exempt from this policy.

1.7 Color and Shade Variation
- Due to differences in screen calibration, actual product color may vary slightly from the images displayed online.
- Minor shade variations are not considered defects. However, if the product delivered is a completely different shade name/number from what was ordered, it qualifies as "Wrong Product Delivered" and is eligible for replacement.
- Foundations and concealers once opened and shade-matched against skin are non-returnable.

1.8 Quality Check and Rejection
- All returned beauty products undergo thorough inspection for seal integrity, fill level, and usage signs.
- Rejection reasons include:
  - Broken or missing protective seal
  - Product usage evident (smudges, finger marks, reduced fill level)
  - Batch number mismatch
  - Missing accessories or outer packaging
  - Return request filed after 7-day window
- Rejected products are returned to the customer with a detailed inspection report.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: CANCELLATION POLICY
----------------------------------------------

2.1 Cancellation Window
- Beauty & Skincare orders can be cancelled within 30 minutes of placement or before the order moves to "Packed" status.
- Once packed, beauty orders cannot be cancelled due to hygiene protocols in warehouse handling.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
----------------------------------------------

3.1 Delivery Timelines
- Standard Delivery: 3-7 business days for most beauty products.
- Temperature-sensitive products (certain serums, vitamin C products, organic/natural products) may be shipped in thermal packaging with faster delivery timelines (1-4 business days) in eligible areas.

3.2 Shipping Charges
- Free shipping on all beauty orders above INR 399.
- Orders below INR 399: Flat fee of INR 49.
- Express delivery available in metro cities at additional cost.

3.3 Packaging for Fragile Products
- Glass bottles, pump dispensers, and aerosol cans are shipped with bubble wrap and protective packaging.
- If inadequate packaging leads to damage, it is our liability, and a free replacement is provided.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: AUTHENTICITY GUARANTEE
----------------------------------------------

4.1 100% Authentic Promise
- All Beauty & Skincare products sold on HelperTom are sourced from authorized distributors, brand directly, or reputable import channels.
- Each product listing specifies whether it is "Brand Authorized" or "Imported by Seller."
- Customers who suspect a counterfeit product can report it to our authenticity verification team. If a product is confirmed counterfeit, a 2X refund of the product value is provided, and the seller faces immediate delisting.

SECTION 5: BEAUTY & SKINCARE FAQ (30 QUESTIONS)
----------------------------------------------

Q1: I received a moisturizer, but the jar is half empty. Can I return it?
A: Yes. If the product appears underfilled or leaked, photograph the jar from multiple angles showing the fill level and report it within 48 hours. If confirmed as a manufacturing defect or transit damage, we will replace it or issue a refund. Note that some products are sold by weight (grams) not volume, and settling may make them appear less full.

Q2: The shade of foundation I ordered looks completely different on my skin. Can I exchange it?
A: If the shade delivered matches the shade name/number on your order, it cannot be returned for shade mismatch as screen colors vary. We recommend using our "Virtual Try-On" feature (where available) or purchasing shade-matching sample cards before committing to a full-size product. If the delivered shade name/number is different from your order, it qualifies as a wrong product and will be replaced.

Q3: I used a new serum for two days, and my face has broken out badly. What do I do?
A: Stop using the product immediately. Take clear photos of the breakout and the product with its batch number visible. Report the reaction within 7 days of delivery. Our team will review your case; for severe reactions, we may request a medical note. Approved cases receive a refund or store credit.

Q4: Can I return a perfume if I don't like its fragrance after spraying once?
A: No. Perfumes, deodorants, and fragrances are non-returnable once the seal is broken due to the volatile and intimate nature of the product. We recommend trying the fragrance at a retail store before purchasing, or buying smaller discovery sets or sample vials first.

Q5: The lipstick I ordered arrived broken at the tip. Is it returnable?
A: Yes. Report it within 48 hours with clear photographs showing the broken tip. We will arrange a replacement. Do not attempt to use or reshape the product as it will then become ineligible.

Q6: What is the return policy for skincare gift sets?
A: Gift sets are returnable only if all products in the set are unopened with all seals intact. If any single product's seal is broken, the entire gift set becomes non-returnable.

Q7: I received an expired sunscreen. How do I claim a refund?
A: Take a clear photo of the expiry date printed on the product packaging and report it within 48 hours. We will process an immediate full refund and blacklist the batch from inventory. In most cases, you will not need to return the expired product.

Q8: Do you accept returns on opened hair care products like shampoos or conditioners?
A: Only if there is a confirmed defect (unusual odor, wrong consistency, contamination). Opened hair care products cannot be returned simply because you didn't like the fragrance or results. We recommend buying smaller trial sizes first.

Q9: I received a face cream with a broken safety seal. Can I return it?
A: Yes. A broken or missing safety seal is considered a packaging defect. Report it immediately upon unboxing with photographic evidence. A replacement will be shipped. Do not use the product.

Q10: Can I exchange a beauty product for a different variant?
A: No. Beauty & Skincare products do not support exchanges for different variants or products. If you receive a wrong variant, it will be replaced with the correct one. You cannot "upgrade" or "switch" to a different product.

Q11: What happens if my nail polish bottle arrives shattered?
A: Due to the flammable nature of nail polish, damaged bottles are a safety hazard. Do not touch the spilled liquid. Photograph the damaged package without opening further. Report within 24 hours. We will process an immediate refund, and no return pickup is needed. Dispose of the damaged item safely.

Q12: I bought a face mask set. One mask was used, and I want to return the rest. Is that possible?
A: No. Multi-mask sets or kits cannot be returned if even one mask or item has been used. The entire set becomes non-returnable once any seal is broken.

Q13: How can I check the authenticity of a high-end beauty product I bought?
A: Many brands offer QR code authentication, hologram verification, or batch code lookup on their websites. If you have doubts, contact HelperTom's Authenticity Team with the batch code, purchase invoice, and product photos. If counterfeit is confirmed, you receive a 2X refund.

Q14: My vitamin C serum arrived slightly yellow. I've read it should be clear. Is this a defect?
A: Vitamin C serums naturally oxidize over time, turning from clear to pale yellow to dark orange/brown. Slight yellowing may be acceptable depending on the manufacturing date. If the serum is dark brown or orange upon arrival, it may have oxidized excessively. Report with batch code photos, and our quality team will assess.

Q15: Can I return a beauty product I received as a free gift with purchase?
A: No. Free gifts, promotional items, and samples are non-returnable and have no cash value. If a free gift arrives damaged, report it for a possible replacement of the gift item alone.

Q16: I ordered a toner but received a cleanser. Can I get both a refund and keep the wrong product?
A: Report "Wrong Product Delivered" within 7 days. We will ship the correct toner. The wrong cleanser will be picked up during the replacement delivery. You cannot keep both products without paying.

Q17: Is there any cooling-off period for beauty purchases like some countries have?
A: No. Indian e-commerce regulations do not mandate a cooling-off period for cosmetics and personal care items. Our return policy is clearly defined and restricted to genuine defects.

Q18: I bought a jade roller and it cracked on first use. Is it covered?
A: Beauty tools (jade rollers, gua sha, facial massagers) fall under a 7-day replacement guarantee for manufacturing defects. If it cracked during normal first use, photograph it and raise a claim. Cracks from drops or impact are not covered.

Q19: What if the batch code on my product doesn't match the box?
A: Report it immediately. Mismatched batch codes may indicate tampering or counterfeit issues. We will investigate and replace or refund the product. This is taken very seriously by our quality team.

Q20: My deodorant can is spraying continuously and won't stop. What should I do?
A: Aerosol malfunction is a defect. Do not attempt to force the nozzle. Record a video showing the malfunction, store the can safely away from heat, and report within 7 days. A replacement or refund will be provided. Do not ship the malfunctioning aerosol back without specific instructions due to safety regulations.

Q21: Can I return a hair styling tool like a straightener or curler if it doesn't work on my hair type?
A: If the product is functioning correctly but not achieving the desired result on your specific hair type, it is not eligible for return. We recommend researching compatibility with your hair type before purchase. If the tool is genuinely defective (doesn't heat up, electrical fault), report within 7 days for replacement.

Q22: What is the return policy for men's grooming products (beard oil, shaving cream)?
A: Men's grooming products follow the same Beauty & Skincare policy. Opened products are non-returnable unless defective. Unopened, sealed products can be returned within 7 days if damaged, defective, or wrong.

Q23: I received a luxury skincare product without its outer box. Is that acceptable?
A: Some luxury brands intentionally sell without outer boxes as part of sustainable packaging initiatives. Check the product page description. If an outer box was explicitly shown and promised and is missing, it may be flagged as incomplete packaging. Report within 48 hours.

Q24: My sheet mask packet was already torn when I opened the outer package. Can I return it?
A: Yes. Photograph the torn packet and report within 48 hours. A replacement sheet mask or refund will be issued. Do not use the mask if the packet integrity is compromised.

Q25: Can I return a beauty product if the ingredient list on the box differs from the website listing?
A: Yes. Ingredient list discrepancies are taken seriously as they may affect allergies or preferences. Report the discrepancy with photos of the actual box's ingredient list and a screenshot of the website listing. We will investigate and offer a refund or replacement.

Q26: I bought a body lotion during a sale. It arrived with the price tag showing a lower MRP. What do I do?
A: Report it with photos. If the invoiced price is higher than the printed MRP, it violates Indian legal metrology rules, and we will refund the difference plus compensation. If the sale price is lower than MRP but the tag shows a different MRP than listed, we will clarify and adjust accordingly.

Q27: Are opened makeup brushes or beauty blenders returnable?
A: No. Makeup brushes, beauty blenders, sponges, and applicators are non-returnable once opened or used due to hygiene reasons. Defective items (shedding bristles, tearing sponges) are replaceable within 7 days.

Q28: What if my face cream causes a burning sensation immediately on application?
A: Wash off immediately with cool water. This could indicate an ingredient sensitivity or a defective batch. Take photos of any visible redness and the product batch code. Report within 7 days. Our support team will prioritize your case, and a refund is highly likely upon review.

Q29: Can I return a partially used sunscreen if I discover the SPF protection is insufficient?
A: SPF efficacy cannot be tested at home. If you believe the product is counterfeit or expired, report with the batch code. However, "insufficient sun protection" based on sunburn experience is subjective and depends on application amount, reapplication frequency, and exposure conditions. These cases are reviewed individually.

Q30: I purchased a night cream, and the texture inside the jar is grainy. Is this normal or defective?
A: Grainy texture can result from temperature fluctuations causing ingredient separation or could indicate an expired/defective product. Photograph the texture clearly and report within 7 days. Mention the batch code. Our quality team will compare with the manufacturer's specifications and determine if it's a defect.
