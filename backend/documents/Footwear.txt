SECTION 1: RETURNS & EXCHANGE POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Footwear products are eligible for return or exchange within 7 days from the date of delivery.
- Both "Change of Mind" returns and "Defect" returns are accepted, subject to the condition requirements below.
- For premium and luxury footwear (above INR 10,000), the return window is extended to 10 days.

1.2 Condition for Returns
- Footwear must be returned in its original, undamaged shoebox with all packaging materials.
- The shoes must be unworn, unwashed, and show no signs of outdoor use.
- All tags, labels, and stickers must be intact and not removed.
- Shoes must be tried on a clean, indoor surface only. Soles showing dirt, gravel marks, or scuffing from outdoor wear will be rejected.
- The original shoebox must not be used as the shipping box. It must be placed inside an outer protective box or polymailer. Shoeboxes with tape directly applied, torn, crushed, or used as the sole shipping container will lead to rejection.
- Insoles, extra laces, dust bags, authenticity cards, care booklets, and any accessories provided must be returned.
- For shoes with attached security tags, the tag must be intact and untampered.

1.3 Exchange Policy
- Exchanges are allowed for a different size of the same model only.
- Exchange requests must be placed within the 7-day return window.
- If the desired size is out of stock, the order is processed as a return with a full refund.
- One free size exchange per order line item. Additional exchanges may incur a shipping fee of INR 99.

1.4 Non-Returnable Footwear
The following footwear categories are non-returnable for hygiene reasons (unless defective):
- Socks (if sold with shoes, the sock pack must remain sealed)
- Insoles and orthotic inserts (once packaging opened)
- Shoe care products (polish, cleaners, waterproofing sprays)

1.5 Defective Footwear
- Defects covered include:
  - Sole separation or delamination
  - Broken or non-functional zippers, buckles, or closures
  - Torn or ripped upper material
  - Stitching coming undone extensively
  - Heel breakage
  - Missing embellishments, studs, or decorative elements
  - Misaligned straps or asymmetry between left and right shoes
  - Significant color bleeding from upper onto socks/skin
  - Waterproof shoes leaking (within the waterproof claim period)
  - Insole detachment or bunching

- Defects NOT covered:
  - Minor glue marks (common in mass-manufactured footwear)
  - Slight color variation from online images
  - Odor from new shoe materials (off-gassing)
  - Squeaky soles (may resolve with break-in)
  - Normal creasing after wear
  - Heel tip wear (these are replaceable consumables)
  - Discomfort or fit preferences (unless the size label is wrong)

1.6 Quality Check for Returns
- Each returned footwear item is inspected for:
  - Sole wear and dirt
  - Upper creasing from walking
  - Interior sock-liner cleanliness
  - Shoebox condition
  - Presence of all accessories
- Products failing inspection are returned to the customer with a report. Return shipping is chargeable.

1.7 Refund Timelines
- Prepaid orders: Refund to source within 5-7 business days after quality check approval.
- Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer within 5-7 business days after bank details are verified.
- Store credit/wallet: Instant upon approval.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: CANCELLATION POLICY
----------------------------------------------

2.1 Cancellation Window
- Footwear orders can be cancelled within 30 minutes of order placement or before packing.
- Customized or personalized footwear (monogrammed, custom colors) cannot be cancelled once production begins, even within 30 minutes. This is noted on the product page.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: WARRANTY
----------------------------------------------

3.1 Warranty Period
- Footwear does not carry a long-term warranty. The 7-day return/exchange window serves as the primary protection.
- Certain premium brands may offer a 30-day to 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects. This information, if applicable, is on the product page and warranty card inside the box.
- Claims beyond 7 days must be pursued with the brand directly per their warranty terms.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: SIZING AND FIT
----------------------------------------------

4.1 Size Charts
- Each product page includes a brand-specific size chart. Customers are strongly advised to measure their feet and compare before ordering.
- Returns due to size mismatch are accepted within the standard 7-day window, subject to condition checks.

4.2 UK/US/EU Size Discrepancy
- The size printed on the shoebox and shoe is the size shipped. If the ordered size (e.g., UK 8) and the delivered size (UK 8) match, size issues are handled as a standard exchange. If the delivered size differs from the ordered size, it is treated as "Wrong Product."

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
----------------------------------------------

5.1 Delivery Timelines
- Standard Delivery: 4-8 business days.
- Premium footwear may ship with priority handling.

5.2 Shipping Charges
- Free shipping on all footwear orders above INR 699.
- Below INR 699: INR 79 flat shipping fee.

5.3 Packaging
- All footwear ships in a protective outer carton to preserve the shoebox. In rare cases of packaging damage during transit, report with photographs for partial compensation or box replacement.

SECTION 6: FOOTWEAR FAQ (30 QUESTIONS)
----------------------------------------------

Q1: I wore the shoes for an hour indoors and decided they don't fit well. Can I still return them?
A: If you wore them only on a clean indoor surface (carpet or clean floor) and the soles show absolutely no signs of wear, dirt, or scuffing, they may pass the quality check. However, any visible creasing, dirt on soles, or sock-liner impressions will lead to rejection. We recommend trying shoes on a carpeted area with thin socks to minimize risk.

Q2: The shoebox got slightly torn when I was opening it. Will my return be rejected?
A: A minor tear may be accepted, but significant damage (crushed corners, ripped lids, tape on the printed surface) will lead to rejection. Always open shoeboxes carefully. If the box was already damaged upon delivery, you should have reported it immediately.

Q3: Can I exchange my sneakers for a completely different color or model?
A: No. Exchanges are only for a different size of the exact same model and color. You cannot switch to a different colorway or style. If you want a different model, return the original for a refund and place a new order.

Q4: I ordered UK 8 but received UK 7. The box says UK 8, but the shoe says UK 7. What now?
A: This is a "Wrong Product Delivered" scenario due to a labeling error. Photograph the shoe size label and the box label showing the discrepancy and report within 7 days. A free replacement with the correct size will be shipped.

Q5: The left shoe feels tighter than the right shoe. Is this a defect?
A: Yes, significant asymmetry between left and right shoes in the same labeled size qualifies as a manufacturing defect. Measure both shoes with a tape and photograph if possible. Report within 7 days for a replacement or refund.

Q6: Can I return shoes if the color looks different in person than on the website?
A: Due to screen calibration differences, minor color variations are expected and not considered defects. If the shoe delivered is a fundamentally different color (e.g., ordered navy blue, received black), report as "Wrong Product." Photographic evidence is required.

Q7: I bought running shoes, but they cause me knee pain. Can I return them?
A: Footwear-induced body pain is subjective and depends on gait, foot type (pronation/supination), and running surface. This is not a product defect and is not covered under the return policy. We recommend gait analysis at a specialty store before purchasing high-performance running shoes.

Q8: What if the sole of my new shoes starts coming off after a week of use?
A: This is a clear manufacturing defect. Report it within the 7-day window with photos of the sole separation. If within the window, a replacement or refund is processed. If beyond 7 days, check if the brand offers a warranty and contact them directly.

Q9: Can I return formal leather shoes that I wore to an event once?
A: No. Worn shoes — even once — show creasing on the leather upper, wear on the sole, and impressions on the insole. These will be rejected during quality check. Buy shoes well in advance to allow for exchange if needed.

Q10: The heel of my new heels broke on the first wear. What should I do?
A: This qualifies as a manufacturing defect. Stop using immediately, photograph the broken heel, and report within the 7-day window. If the incident occurred due to a fall or external impact, it may not be covered. Be honest about the circumstances.

Q11: I want to exchange my sandals for a half-size bigger. Is that possible?
A: If the model is available in half-sizes and the size you want is in stock, yes. Exchange requests for size changes are allowed within 7 days, provided the sandals are in pristine, unworn condition.

Q12: Do I need to return the free pair of socks that came with my shoes?
A: If the socks were a free promotional gift and are still sealed, they do not necessarily need to be returned. However, if the socks were part of a combo deal with a combined price, the entire package must be returned unopened.

Q13: My new suede boots got water stains from light rain. Can I return them?
A: No. Suede is a delicate material that water stains easily. This is not a defect but a characteristic of the material. We recommend applying a suede protector spray before wearing suede footwear outdoors. Stained items are not returnable.

Q14: The shoes I received have a strong chemical/glue smell. Is this normal?
A: Yes, a strong odor from new shoes is common due to adhesives and synthetic materials used in manufacturing. This "off-gassing" dissipates with airing out. It is not considered a defect unless the smell is unusually pungent and persists for weeks.

Q15: What counts as an "unworn" shoe for return purposes?
A: An unworn shoe shows:
- No creasing on the toe box or vamp
- No dirt, pebbles, or wear marks on the outsole
- Clean, crisp sock-liner without foot impressions or discoloration
- Tags and labels intact
- Pristine shoebox without tape or shipping labels directly on it

Q16: I received a pair of flip-flops, and the strap came out of the sole on first wear. Is it defective?
A: Yes. Flip-flop strap detachment is a manufacturing defect. Photograph and report within 7 days. Do not attempt to glue it back, as this voids the return eligibility.

Q17: Can I return safety shoes or work boots if they don't meet my workplace standards?
A: Safety shoes with specific certifications (steel toe, electrical hazard, slip resistance) are manufactured to standards. If the certified standard is printed on the shoe and matches what was ordered, personal workplace requirements are not grounds for return. If the certification is missing or misrepresented, it qualifies as a defect.

Q18: What if the shoes I ordered have a slight scratch on the leather out of the box?
A: Minor cosmetic imperfections like light surface scratches can occur during manufacturing and shipping. If the scratch is deep, long, or obvious, report within 48 hours with photos. The quality team will assess if it crosses the threshold for a defect.

Q19: I want to return a pair of kids' shoes that my child outgrew before wearing. They are completely new and unworn. Can I?
A: If the shoes are in absolutely new condition with the box, tags, and packaging intact, and it's within 7 days, you can initiate a return. The condition check will be the final decider.

Q20: Are custom-printed or personalized sneakers returnable?
A: No. Customized, monogrammed, or personalized footwear is made to order and is non-returnable and non-exchangeable unless there is a manufacturing defect. This is clearly stated on the product page before ordering.

Q21: The insoles of my new shoes started bunching up after two wears. What can I do?
A: Insole bunching or detachment is a defect. Report within 7 days with photos. After 7 days, you can purchase replacement insoles, which are typically considered consumable parts.

Q22: I'm between sizes. Can I order two sizes and return the one that doesn't fit?
A: Yes, you can order multiple sizes and return the one that doesn't fit, provided all conditions are met (unworn, box intact, 7-day window). Note that repeated bulk-ordering with high returns may flag your account for review.

Q23: What is the return process for footwear purchased during End of Season Sale?
A: Sale items follow the same 7-day return policy unless explicitly marked "Final Sale - No Returns" or "Clearance - Non-Returnable." Check the product page carefully. Deeply discounted items (70%+ off) may be marked as final sale.

Q24: My shoes squeak loudly with every step. Is this covered?
A: Squeaking can result from sole material, moisture, or break-in period. Many squeaks resolve after a few wears. If the squeaking is severe and persists, report within 7 days with a video. It will be assessed case by case. Squeaking alone is often not considered a defect.

Q25: The buckle on my sandals doesn't stay fastened. Can I return them?
A: A non-functional buckle is a clear defect. Report within 7 days with a video showing the buckle failing. A replacement or refund will be provided.

Q26: I received a branded shoebox but the shoes inside seem to be a different brand. What do I do?
A: This is an extremely serious issue suggesting counterfeiting or warehouse error. Report immediately. Photograph everything clearly. We will investigate urgently and offer a full refund plus a goodwill credit for the inconvenience.

Q27: Can I return crocs or clogs if they feel too loose?
A: Crocs and similar clogs are designed for a relaxed fit. If the size ordered matches what you received, fit preference returns follow standard policy — unworn condition only. If the sizing feels significantly off, check the size chart; Crocs sizing differs from standard shoe sizing.

Q28: My football cleats have studs that are already loosening after one match. Is this a defect?
A: Yes, loosening studs after one use indicate a manufacturing defect. Report immediately within 7 days. Do not continue using them as this may worsen the damage. Photograph the loose studs clearly.

Q29: What should I do if my return pickup person refuses to accept the package because the shoebox is damaged?
A: The pickup agent collects all packages as long as they are packed. Rejection happens at the warehouse quality check, not at pickup. If an agent refuses pickup, report to customer support immediately for rescheduling. Make sure the shoebox is placed inside a protective outer package.

Q30: I received vegan leather shoes but I suspect they are real leather. How can I verify and what can I do?
A: This is a material misrepresentation issue. If you have a genuine basis for suspicion, report it with the product page screenshot and your concerns. We take material claims (vegan, leather-free, sustainable) very seriously. Verified misrepresentation results in a full refund and product delisting review.
