SECTION 1: RETURNS & REFUND POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Luxury Fashion (Designer Apparel, Bags, Shoes, Accessories): 10 days from the date of delivery.
- Luxury Watches and Fine Jewelry: 10 days from delivery (subject to strict verification).
- Luxury Beauty and Fragrances: 10 days from delivery (unopened with seals intact only).
- Luxury Home Decor and Lifestyle Products: 10 days from delivery.
- Limited Edition and Collectible Items: 10 days from delivery.
- Made-to-Order and Bespoke Luxury Items: Non-returnable once production begins.

1.2 Condition for Returns — The Pristine Standard
All luxury product returns must meet the "Pristine Standard" — a stricter condition check than regular categories:
- The product must be in absolutely unused, unworn, unaltered condition.
- All original packaging must be returned, including but not limited to:
  - Branded outer box and inner box
  - Dust bags, fabric pouches, and protective covers
  - Authenticity cards, certificates, and warranty booklets
  - Care instruction booklets and brand literature
  - Original tissue paper, ribbons, and packaging fillers
  - Protective films on hardware, screens, and mirrors
  - Security tags, brand tags, and barcode stickers (must be intact and attached)
- For handbags and leather goods:
  - All protective felt covers on hardware must be in place.
  - No scratches, fingerprints, or patina on hardware.
  - No stretching of handles, straps, or leather from handling.
  - Interior must be completely untouched — no marks, no odors, no items placed inside.
  - The bag's shape must be maintained with original stuffing/pillows.
- For footwear:
  - Must have absolutely no sole wear, not even on the bottom from trying on a hard floor.
  - Try luxury shoes only on carpeted surfaces with protective sole covers if provided.
  - Red soles (Christian Louboutin, etc.) must be flawless — any scuffing leads to rejection.
  - Original shoebox, dust bags, and authentication materials must be in mint condition.
- For watches:
  - All protective films on the case, crystal, and bracelet must be intact.
  - The crown stopper must be in place.
  - Bracelet must not be resized. Any removed links must be present.
  - Watch must not have been wound or set (automatic winding from handling is acceptable).
  - No scratches on the case, crystal, or clasp from trying on.

1.3 Non-Returnable Luxury Products
The following luxury items are non-returnable under any circumstances (except manufacturing defect):
- Custom-made, bespoke, and monogrammed luxury items
- Fine jewelry where the security seal is broken (earrings, nose pins, and all piercing jewelry are non-returnable)
- Luxury fragrances with broken cellophane wrap or opened box
- Luxury beauty and skincare products with broken seals
- Lingerie, swimwear, and intimate apparel (hygiene)
- Auctions and pre-owned/vintage luxury items (sold as-is with detailed condition reports)
- Limited edition items where the serial number or hologram has been tampered with
- Items where the brand's original security tag has been removed or tampered with
- Luxury watches where the case back has been opened

1.4 Authentication and Verification
- Every luxury product shipped by HelperTom undergoes multi-point authentication before dispatch.
- Returned luxury items undergo a rigorous 14-point authentication and condition verification process:
  1. Brand logo and font verification
  2. Stitching pattern and thread quality analysis
  3. Hardware engraving and weight verification
  4. Zipper brand and functionality check
  5. Serial number, date code, and hologram verification
  6. Material quality and texture assessment
  7. RFID chip scan (where applicable)
  8. Packaging and documentation authenticity
  9. Weight comparison against original dispatch record
  10. Odor assessment (perfume, smoke, storage odors)
  11. UV light inspection for hidden marks or alterations
  12. Microscope inspection of engravings and hallmarks
  13. Comparison with original dispatch photographs
  14. Third-party authentication (for select ultra-luxury items)

- The authentication and verification process takes 7-10 business days from the date of pickup.
- If the product fails authentication (suspected counterfeit switch, alteration, or damage), the return is rejected, and the product is returned to the customer. Legal action may be pursued for suspected fraud.
- If the product passes authentication and condition verification, the refund is processed within 3-5 business days.

1.5 Refund Timelines
- Prepaid orders: Total 10-15 business days from pickup to refund completion.
- Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 10-15 business days after verification.
- For ultra-luxury items (above INR 5,00,000), the refund may take up to 20 business days due to enhanced verification.

1.6 Anti-Counterfeit and Fraud Prevention
- HelperTom maintains a zero-tolerance policy for return fraud on luxury items.
- Customers are advised to record a complete unboxing video for all luxury purchases, showing the package seal, shipping label, and product inspection.
- Returned luxury items are photographed at every stage of the return process.
- Customers with a history of suspicious luxury returns may be flagged, and future luxury purchases may require additional verification or may be restricted.

SECTION 5: LUXURY FAQ (30 QUESTIONS)
----------------------------------------------

Q1: I bought a designer handbag, but the leather has a slight scratch. It was there when I unboxed it. What should I do?
A: If the scratch was present at unboxing, photograph it clearly with the unboxing video as evidence and report within 48 hours. The bag will be inspected. If confirmed as a pre-existing defect (not customer-inflicted), a replacement or refund will be provided. This is why unboxing videos are essential for luxury purchases.

Q2: Can I return a luxury watch if I wore it for a day and decided it's too heavy for my wrist?
A: No. Any wear on a luxury watch — even from a single day — will show micro-scratches on the polished surfaces, strap creasing, or bracelet stretching. Luxury watch returns require all protective films to be intact. Fit and weight should be researched thoroughly or tried at a boutique before purchasing.

Q3: I received my designer shoes, and the red sole got a tiny scuff just from me trying them on carpet. Will this affect my return?
A: Yes. Even a minor scuff on a lacquered sole (like Louboutin) will lead to return rejection. Luxury shoes should only be tried on with sole-protective films in place or on an absolutely clean, soft surface. If the scuff occurred despite careful handling, it may be covered as a delicate finish issue — report with detailed photos for case-by-case review.

Q4: What if the serial number on my luxury bag doesn't match the authenticity card?
A: This is a serious discrepancy. Photograph both the bag's serial number (inside tag/stamp) and the authenticity card showing the mismatch. Report within 48 hours. Do not use the bag. This will be treated as a potential counterfeit or packaging error, and a full investigation will be launched. You will receive a replacement or full refund.

Q5: Can I return a luxury perfume if I opened the cellophane and sprayed it once to test the scent?
A: No. Luxury fragrances are non-returnable once the cellophane wrap is removed or the box is opened. Fragrance is highly personal. We strongly recommend testing the scent at a department store or purchasing a discovery set/sample vial before committing to a full bottle.

Q6: I ordered a luxury silk scarf, and there's a small pull in the fabric straight out of the box. Is this returnable?
A: Yes. A fabric pull or snag on a new luxury silk scarf is a defect. Photograph the pull clearly, showing it against the packaging. Report within 48 hours. A replacement scarf will be shipped. Silk is delicate, and manufacturing/packaging defects do occur.

Q7: My luxury order arrived with the security seal already broken. What should I do?
A: Do not accept the package if you notice the security seal is broken before signing. If you discover it after accepting, immediately photograph the broken seal and the package exterior. Report within 2 hours of delivery. We take broken security seals extremely seriously as it may indicate tampering. A full investigation will be conducted.

Q8: Can I exchange a luxury bag for a different color if I change my mind?
A: Exchanges for different colors or models are generally not supported for luxury items due to authentication and inventory constraints. You would need to return the original bag (if it passes the Pristine Standard) and place a new order for the desired color.

Q9: I bought a limited edition luxury item. The number on the item (e.g., 150/500) is different from what I saw in the listing photo. Is this normal?
A: Limited edition listing photos are often representative; the specific number you receive may vary unless the exact number was listed as the one for sale. This is normal and not a defect. If the listing promised a specific edition number and delivered a different one, report as "Wrong Product."

Q10: My luxury watch stopped working after 3 days. I didn't drop it or expose it to magnets. What now?
A: Report within the 10-day return window. A non-functional luxury watch is covered as a manufacturing defect. Do not attempt to wind it forcefully or open the case back. The watch will be inspected by our certified horologist. If confirmed as a movement defect, a replacement or refund will be provided.

Q11: Can I return a luxury belt if I tried it on and the buckle left a slight mark on the leather?
A: If the buckle has marked the leather from a single try-on, the belt may fail the Pristine Standard inspection. Luxury belts should be tried on minimally, without fully securing the buckle, if you're unsure about keeping them. Minor marks may lead to rejection.

Q12: I received a designer dress, and the fabric has a slight variation in color from the product image. Is this a defect?
A: Minor variations in color due to lighting and screen calibration are expected, especially for luxury fabrics with depth and texture (silk, velvet, jacquard). If the color is fundamentally different (e.g., ordered navy, received black), report with photos showing the dress in natural light against the order. Significant discrepancies are treated as wrong product.

Q13: What happens if my returned luxury item gets lost in transit during the return process?
A: All luxury returns are fully insured for their full retail value. If the return shipment is lost, you will receive a full refund. We use premium, tracked, and insured logistics for all luxury returns.

Q14: I bought a pre-owned vintage luxury watch. It arrived with a different strap than described. What do I do?
A: Pre-owned luxury items are sold with detailed condition reports and exact photographs. If the strap differs from what was shown and described, report within 48 hours. This is a listing error. A partial refund, replacement strap, or full return (as per the listing terms) will be arranged.

Q15: Can I return a luxury candle if I lit it once and didn't like the scent throw?
A: No. Once a luxury candle has been lit, it is non-returnable. Scent throw depends on room size, burn time, and personal sensitivity. Cold sniffing (smelling the unlit candle) gives a good indication of the fragrance.

Q16: The diamond on my luxury ring looks cloudy compared to the certification photo. Is this a quality issue?
A: Diamonds can appear cloudy due to fluorescence, inclusions, or simply needing cleaning after handling. Clean the diamond gently with a soft cloth. If cloudiness persists and significantly differs from the certification grade, report within 10 days. The ring will be re-evaluated by a gemologist. If the stone is found to be misrepresented, a full refund or replacement will be provided.

Q17: I ordered a luxury wallet, and the stitching on one corner is slightly uneven. Is this a defect or "character"?
A: Hand-stitched luxury goods may have slight variations that are considered artisanal character. However, if the stitching is significantly uneven, loose, or deviates visibly from the product's design standard, it is a defect. Report with close-up photos. Our quality team will compare against the brand's craftsmanship standards.

Q18: Can I cancel a made-to-order luxury sofa after it's already in production?
A: Cancellation after production begins incurs a significant cancellation fee (typically 30-50% of the product value) to cover materials, artisan labor, and production costs already incurred. Check your order's specific cancellation terms. Contact support to check the production status before requesting cancellation.

Q19: My luxury sunglasses arrived with a loose lens. The box was undamaged. What should I do?
A: A loose lens is a manufacturing defect. Do not attempt to press it back in, as you may damage the frame. Report within 48 hours with a photo. A replacement pair will be shipped.

Q20: I received a luxury shawl with a faint perfume odor. It smells like it was tried on or returned before. Is this acceptable?
A: No. Luxury items must arrive in absolutely pristine, new condition. Any odor (perfume, smoke, storage) on a "new" luxury item is unacceptable. Report within 48 hours. A replacement or refund will be provided. This is a quality control failure on our part.

Q21: Can I return a luxury pen that I inked once to test the nib?
A: No. Once a fountain pen has been inked, it is considered used and is non-returnable. Nib smoothness and ink flow can be tested at authorized retailers before purchasing. Some brands offer nib exchange programs directly.

Q22: My luxury handbag's hardware has tarnished slightly after a week in its dust bag. I haven't used it. Is this a defect?
A: Some hardware finishes, particularly on certain designer brands, can develop a patina or light tarnish even in storage due to humidity. This is often considered a characteristic of the material (e.g., uncoated brass). If the tarnishing is excessive or occurred despite proper storage, report within 10 days with photos. Cases are evaluated individually.

Q23: I bought a limited edition sneaker, and the box arrived crushed. The shoes are fine. Can I get a replacement box?
A: For collectible sneakers, the box is part of the product's value. A crushed box is considered damage. Report within 48 hours with photos of the crushed box. We will attempt to source a replacement box. If unsuccessful, a partial refund for the diminished collectible value may be offered, or a full return.

Q24: What is the authentication guarantee on luxury products?
A: HelperTom guarantees 100% authenticity on all luxury products. Each item is authenticated by our team of experts and, for select items, by third-party authentication services. If any product is independently verified as counterfeit by a recognized authority, we provide a 3X refund of the product value, plus covering the authentication cost.

Q25: Can I return a luxury tie if I don't like the width after seeing it in person?
A: Yes, if the tie is unaltered, unworn, with all tags and packaging intact, and returned within 10 days. Tie width is a personal preference and a valid change-of-mind return reason if the item is in pristine condition.

Q26: My luxury watch came with a warranty card that's not stamped or dated. Is this valid?
A: Some brands have moved to digital warranty activation, while others still require a stamped and dated physical card. Check the warranty information on the product page. If the card should be stamped per the brand's policy and it's blank, report within 48 hours. We will facilitate getting the warranty properly activated.

Q27: I ordered a designer necklace, and the clasp is extremely difficult to open and close. My fingernail broke trying. Is this a defect?
A: A clasp that is excessively stiff to the point of being non-functional or causing injury is a defect. Report within 10 days with a description. Do not force it with tools, as this will cause damage. A replacement or repair will be arranged.

Q28: Can I return a luxury product if I found a better price elsewhere after purchasing?
A: HelperTom does not offer a price match guarantee for luxury items. If the product is returnable under the Pristine Standard (unused, all packaging), you can return it within 10 days and purchase from the other retailer. Ensure you do not use or damage the item.

Q29: The exotic leather (crocodile) on my bag has a small scale that's lifted. Is this natural or a defect?
A: Exotic leathers have natural scale patterns and occasional lifted scales, which are characteristics of the material and often considered a sign of authenticity. A single slightly lifted scale is normal. However, if scales are peeling off or there's significant flaking, it's a defect. Report with macro photos for expert evaluation.

Q30: I'm gifting a luxury watch. What is the return window for the recipient if their birthday is a month after my purchase?
A: The return window starts from the date of delivery, not the date of gifting. If you're purchasing in advance, consider the timing. For gifts, we recommend purchasing closer to the gifting date. You can also contact our luxury concierge to inquire about extended holiday return windows that may be available during festive seasons.
