SECTION 1: RETURNS & REPLACEMENT POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Books: 7 days from the date of delivery (Replacement Only for defective/damaged/wrong items).
- Stationery: 7 days from the date of delivery.
- Educational Materials and Study Guides: 7 days from delivery.
- Exam Preparation Books: 7 days from delivery (replacement for defective/wrong items).
- Digital Products (eBooks, Audiobooks): Non-returnable and non-refundable once downloaded or accessed.
- Print-on-Demand Books: Non-returnable unless damaged or misprinted.

1.2 Book Return Policy (Replacement Only Model)
- Books follow a "Replacement Only" policy for valid reasons. Monetary refunds for books are not provided unless the exact title/edition is out of stock or discontinued.
- Valid reasons for replacement:
  - Damaged book: Torn pages, water damage, crushed spine, missing pages, blurred/unreadable print
  - Wrong book delivered: Different title, author, edition, or language from order
  - Defective printing: Blank pages, pages out of order, upside-down pages, smeared ink
  - Missing components: Access codes, CD-ROMs, maps, pull-outs explicitly listed as included
- Invalid reasons for return/replacement:
  - "I don't like the book content" or "Not what I expected"
  - "Found it cheaper elsewhere"
  - "Bought by mistake" or "Changed my mind after reading"
  - "Don't need it anymore"
  - Minor cover scuffs from normal handling during shipping
  - Slight variations in cover design from online image (publishers update covers periodically)

1.3 Stationery Return Policy
- Stationery items are returnable within 7 days if:
  - Damaged upon delivery: Broken pens, dried-out markers, cracked rulers, torn notebooks
  - Wrong product: Different color, type, brand, or quantity delivered
  - Manufacturing defect: Pens that don't write, markers with missing ink, notebooks with missing pages
  - Leaking pens, highlighters, or ink bottles
  - Expired products (e.g., correction fluid, glue with past expiry date)

- Non-returnable stationery (hygiene/use concerns):
  - Opened packs of pens, pencils, markers, or crayons (unless defective)
  - Used notebooks, registers, or diaries with any writing or marks
  - Opened art supplies: paints, inks, canvases (unless defective)
  - Erasers, sharpeners, and small consumables once packaging opened
  - Stickers, stamps, and sticker books with any used stickers
  - Opened adhesives: glue sticks, fevicol, tapes

1.4 Condition for Returns
- Books must be returned in the same condition as received. No highlighting, underlining, writing, dog-eared pages, or stains.
- Stationery must be in original packaging with all seals intact (unless opened to discover a defect).
- Notebooks and registers must have no pen or pencil marks, torn pages, or creased covers.
- Art supplies must have factory seals intact; partially used supplies are non-returnable.
- Exam preparation books with activated online access codes are non-returnable.

1.5 Replacement Process for Books
- Step 1: Customer raises a replacement request within 7 days, specifying the damage/defect with photographs.
- Step 2: A return pickup is scheduled. The book is collected.
- Step 3: The book undergoes a condition check (2-3 business days).
- Step 4: Upon confirmation, a brand-new copy of the same title and edition is shipped (2-5 business days).
- Step 5: If the exact title/edition is out of stock or discontinued, a full refund is processed.
- Step 6: If the returned book shows customer-inflicted damage (coffee stains, torn pages, writing), replacement is rejected, and the book is returned to the customer.

1.6 Refund Timelines (When Applicable)
- Refunds for books are processed only when replacement is not possible.
- Refund to source: 5-7 business days after replacement cancellation confirmation.
- Stationery defect refunds: 5-7 business days after quality check.

SECTION 2: CANCELLATION POLICY
----------------------------------------------

2.1 Cancellation Window
- Books and Stationery: Cancellation within 30 minutes of order placement or before "Packed" status.
- Pre-orders (upcoming book releases): Cancellation up to 48 hours before the release/shipping date.
- Print-on-Demand books: Cancellation within 24 hours of order placement (before printing begins).

2.2 Post-Window Cancellation
- Standard orders: Once packed, cancellation is not possible. Wait for delivery and follow the return policy if eligible.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: DIGITAL AND ELECTRONIC PRODUCTS
----------------------------------------------

3.1 eBooks and Audiobooks
- Digital products are delivered instantly via email or in-app access.
- Once the download link is accessed or the file is downloaded, the product is non-returnable and non-refundable.
- Accidental purchases of digital products are not refunded. Please verify the title and format before clicking "Buy."
- Pre-ordered eBooks: Refundable only until 48 hours before the release date.

3.2 Access Codes and Online Resources
- Textbooks and study guides often include single-use access codes for online portals.
- Once the access code is revealed or activated, the entire book/package becomes non-returnable.
- If the access code is missing or already used (invalid), it is treated as a "Missing Component" defect and is eligible for replacement.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: TEXTBOOK AND ACADEMIC MATERIALS POLICY
----------------------------------------------

4.1 Edition Changes
- Publishers frequently update editions. The edition shipped matches the ISBN listed on the product page.
- If an older edition is received despite a newer ISBN being ordered, it qualifies as "Wrong Product."
- If the correct ISBN is delivered but the customer expected a different edition, it is not covered. Always verify the ISBN and edition year before ordering.

4.2 Competitive Exam Books
- Exam books with current affairs sections: The edition year and content coverage are as listed.
- Outdated material (e.g., last year's exam pattern when this year's was ordered) due to a listing error qualifies as a defect.
- Solved papers and sample papers: Once written in, they are non-returnable.

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
----------------------------------------------

5.1 Delivery Timelines
- Standard Books and Stationery: 3-7 business days.
- Pre-orders: Shipped on or after the official release date.
- Imported/International Editions: 7-15 business days.
- Print-on-Demand: 5-10 business days (printing + shipping).

5.2 Shipping Charges
- Free shipping on all book orders above INR 399.
- Below INR 399: Flat shipping fee of INR 49.
- Stationery orders: Free shipping above INR 499; INR 49 below that.

5.3 Packaging Standards
- Books are shipped in protective cardboard mailers or bubble-wrapped envelopes.
- Hardcovers receive additional corner protection.
- Damage caused by inadequate packaging is our liability. Report with photos.

SECTION 6: BOOKS & STATIONERY FAQ (30 QUESTIONS)
----------------------------------------------

Q1: I received a book with a torn dust jacket. The book itself is fine. Can I get a replacement?
A: Yes, the dust jacket is part of the book product. Photograph the tear and report within 7 days. A replacement with an intact dust jacket will be shipped. For minor tears, we may offer a partial refund instead of a full replacement.

Q2: The novel I ordered has smudged print on several pages, making it unreadable. What should I do?
A: Photograph the smudged pages clearly and report within 7 days. This is a printing defect. A replacement copy will be shipped after quality check. Do not attempt to read or handle the book extensively until the return is processed.

Q3: I bought a textbook for my college course, but the professor changed the syllabus. Can I return it?
A: No. Syllabus changes by your institution are not a valid return reason. We recommend confirming the required textbook list with your professor before purchasing. The book is not defective; it simply no longer fits your personal requirements.

Q4: Can I return a novel after reading it because I didn't enjoy the story?
A: No. Books cannot be returned based on content satisfaction. This policy exists to prevent the platform from becoming a free library. Book reviews, synopses, and sample chapters are available to help you decide before purchasing.

Q5: I ordered a paperback, but I received the hardcover edition. Is that a problem?
A: If you received a higher-value edition (hardcover instead of paperback), you can choose to keep it at no extra charge or request the correct paperback edition. If you received a lower-value edition, report as "Wrong Product" for replacement with the correct edition.

Q6: The access code inside my textbook has already been scratched off and used. What now?
A: Photograph the revealed code and report within 7 days. This qualifies as a defective/missing component. A replacement textbook with a fresh, valid access code will be shipped. Do not wait beyond the return window to check access codes.

Q7: I bought a set of sketch pens, and one color is completely dried out. Can I return just that pen?
A: For multi-color sets, we typically replace the entire set. Report with a photo showing the dried-out pen's tip and a scribble test. A replacement set will be shipped. Single defective items in a set are not replaced individually unless the item is sold as individual units.

Q8: Can I return a diary that I wrote one page in and then realized I don't like the paper quality?
A: No. A diary with any writing, however minimal, is considered used and is non-returnable. Paper quality specifications (GSM, ruled/unruled) are listed on the product page. Review these before purchasing.

Q9: I received a completely different book than what I ordered. The package had my order slip but the wrong book inside.
A: This is a clear "Wrong Product" case. Photograph the wrong book next to the packing slip and report within 7 days. The correct book will be shipped, and the wrong book will be picked up. Do not start reading the wrong book.

Q10: My imported book has a different cover design than shown on the website. Is it the same book?
A: International editions often have different cover art from the Indian or US editions. Check the ISBN on the book against the ISBN on your order. If the ISBNs match, it is the correct book despite the cover variation. Cover changes are not defects.

Q11: Can I return an opened box of crayons because the colors are not as vibrant as expected?
A: No. Color vibrancy is subjective and varies by brand. Opened crayon boxes cannot be returned. We recommend buying a smaller pack to test a brand's quality before purchasing larger sets.

Q12: I ordered a book as a gift, and it arrived with a bent corner. Can I get a fresh copy?
A: Yes. Cosmetic damage like bent corners, crushed spines, or scuffed covers qualifies for replacement. Photograph the damage and report within 7 days. We understand that gifts need to be in pristine condition.

Q13: The fountain pen I ordered skips and doesn't write smoothly. Is this a defect?
A: Fountain pens often require a "break-in" period and proper ink saturation. Clean the nib with warm water and try again. If the problem persists, report within 7 days with a video showing the skipping. A replacement or refund will be provided.

Q14: I pre-ordered a book 3 months ago. Now I don't want it. Can I cancel and get a full refund?
A: Yes. Pre-orders can be cancelled up to 48 hours before the scheduled release/shipping date with a full refund. After that, the standard cancellation window applies.

Q15: Can I return a coloring book if I started coloring one page and didn't like it?
A: No. Any coloring, drawing, or writing in a book makes it non-returnable. The product is considered used once any page is marked.

Q16: I bought an eBook by mistake. I meant to buy the paperback. Can I get a refund?
A: No. Digital products like eBooks and audiobooks are non-refundable once purchased and accessed. The format is clearly labeled on the product page. Always double-check the format before completing the purchase.

Q17: My order included a free bookmark, but it wasn't in the package. Can I claim it?
A: Free promotional items like bookmarks, posters, and bookplates are subject to availability and have no cash value. Their absence is not a valid reason for return or refund. However, you can mention it to support, and we will try to include one in your next order.

Q18: The textbook I ordered has pages 45-60 missing. The pages jump from 44 to 61. What do I do?
A: This is a significant printing/binding defect. Photograph the page jump clearly and report within 7 days. A replacement will be shipped immediately. This is a clear manufacturing error.

Q19: Can I return an opened pack of sticky notes because the adhesive is weak?
A: Yes. Weak or non-sticky adhesive on sticky notes is a manufacturing defect. Photograph a note falling off a surface and report within 7 days. A replacement pack will be shipped.

Q20: I ordered a competitive exam book, and many answers in the answer key are wrong. Is this returnable?
A: If there are confirmed factual errors in the answer key that go beyond occasional typographical errors, this qualifies as a content defect. Report with specific examples of wrong answers and the correct references. Upon verification, a refund or replacement with the corrected edition will be provided.

Q21: My brand new book has pages printed upside down and in reverse order. It looks like a misprint.
A: This is a rare but legitimate printing defect. Photograph the upside-down/reversed pages. Report within 7 days. A replacement copy will be shipped. Misprinted copies are sometimes sought by collectors, but we will replace it for you.

Q22: Can I return a set of watercolor paints if the colors are not true to the packaging?
A: Color representation on packaging is approximate. If the paint quality is poor (chalky, doesn't dissolve, separated pigments), it may be a defect. Report within 7 days. Opened art supplies are assessed case by case.

Q23: I bought a book that was marked "Signed by Author" but the copy I received has no signature. What do I do?
A: Photograph the title page showing the absence of a signature and report within 7 days. A "Signed Copy" is a specific product variant. If unsigned, it is "Wrong Product." A signed copy will be sent, or a full refund provided if signed copies are no longer available.

Q24: Can I exchange a book for a different title by the same author?
A: No. Exchanges are only for the exact same title and edition. You cannot swap one book for another. Return the book if eligible and place a new order.

Q25: My child's activity book came with a sticker sheet that has stickers already peeled off. Is this returnable?
A: Yes. Missing or used stickers in a "new" activity book is a defect. Report within 7 days with photos. A replacement will be shipped. Do not attempt to use the remaining stickers.

Q26: I received a book with water damage. The pages are wavy, and there are water stains. What now?
A: Water damage is a clear defect. Photograph the wavy pages and stains and report within 7 days. A replacement will be shipped. This usually happens during transit or warehouse storage and is fully covered.

Q27: I bought a box of pencils, and the leads keep breaking when I sharpen them. Is the whole box defective?
A: Leads breaking repeatedly often indicate the pencils were dropped during transit, causing internal lead fractures. Try 2-3 pencils. If all break consistently, photograph the broken leads and report within 7 days. A replacement box will be shipped.

Q28: Can I return a GRE/GMAT prep book if the online test access code has expired?
A: If the access code expiry date was not disclosed and it expired before a reasonable use period, report within 7 days. Expired access codes are treated as a defect. If the expiry date was clearly mentioned on the product page, it is not covered.

Q29: I ordered a book in Hindi but received the English version. What should I do?
A: Language is a fundamental product attribute. Photograph the English cover and report within 7 days as "Wrong Product." The Hindi edition will be shipped. This is a warehouse picking error and is fully covered.

Q30: Can I return a book if I found typographical errors in the text?
A: Typographical errors are the publisher's responsibility, not the retailer's. A few minor typos are common and not a valid return reason. If the book has excessive errors to the point of being unreadable, report with specific examples. We will forward the issue to the publisher and may offer a refund on a case-by-case basis.
