SECTION 1: RETURNS & REFUND POLICY
----------------------------------------------

1.1 Return Window
- Furniture: 7 days from the date of delivery for returns and replacements.
- Large Furniture (Sofas, Beds, Wardrobes, Dining Tables): 10 days from the date of delivery.
- Home Decor (Lamps, Vases, Mirrors, Clocks, Showpieces): 7 days from the date of delivery.
- Mattresses: 10 days return window. Some brands offer a 30 to 100-night sleep trial; check the product page for trial eligibility.
- Custom-Made and Made-to-Order Furniture: Non-returnable once production begins.

1.2 Condition for Returns
- Furniture must be returned in its original condition, undamaged, and without any signs of assembly or use.
- Products that require assembly and have been assembled (even partially) are non-returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect.
- Disassembled returns must include all hardware (screws, bolts, Allen keys, dowels) and original packaging.
- Upholstered furniture must be free of stains, pet hair, odors, and fabric damage.
- Mattresses must be unopened or, if opened under a sleep trial, in clean condition with no stains, tears, or odors. Opened mattresses are collected under specific brand trial programs only.
- All product tags, labels, and law labels (on mattresses and upholstered items) must be intact.

1.3 Defective or Damaged Furniture
- Defects covered:
  - Structural damage: cracked wood, broken joints, bent metal frames
  - Upholstery defects: torn fabric, loose stitching, uneven cushion filling
  - Finish defects: scratches, dents, peeling veneer, uneven paint/stain
  - Glass/mirror damage: cracks, chips, shattered glass upon delivery
  - Missing hardware or parts preventing assembly
  - Wobbling or instability due to uneven legs or frame
  - Incorrect dimensions (deviation >5% from listed dimensions)
  - Color/finish significantly different from product images

- Defects NOT covered:
  - Natural wood grain variations, knots, and color variations (characteristics of solid wood)
  - Minor color variations between online images and actual product (screen calibration)
  - Slight variations in dimensions (up to 5% or ±2 inches for large furniture)
  - Creaking or settling sounds from new furniture (normal for solid wood and new upholstery)
  - Leather variations (natural wrinkles, scars, color variations in genuine leather)
  - Handmade product irregularities (characteristic of artisan work)
  - Cushion softening over time (normal compression with use)

1.4 Delivery Inspection (Open Delivery)
- All Furniture and large Home Decor items are delivered via "Open Delivery."
- The delivery team will unbox and place the product in the customer's chosen location.
- The customer (or a representative) must be present to inspect the product for:
  - Visible damage, scratches, dents
  - Correct model, color, size
  - Functionality (drawers, doors, mechanisms)
- Any damage or defect must be noted on the Proof of Delivery (POD) document before signing.
- If damage is identified during Open Delivery, the customer can:
  - Refuse delivery (full refund initiated)
  - Accept delivery and request a replacement part or repair
  - Accept delivery with a partial refund for cosmetic damage
- Once the POD is signed with "Received in Good Condition," claims for visible damage become harder to process.

1.5 Post-Delivery Damage Discovery
- Concealed damage (discovered after the delivery team leaves) must be reported within 48 hours with clear photographic evidence.
- Manufacturing defects discovered after assembly (by HelperTom-authorized assembly team) are covered under the 7/10-day window.
- Self-assembled furniture damage during assembly is NOT covered. We strongly recommend professional assembly through our authorized service.

1.6 Assembly Service
- HelperTom offers professional assembly services for select furniture items.
- Assembly charges, if applicable, are displayed at checkout.
- If the assembly team damages the product during installation, it is replaced at no cost.
- If the assembly team identifies a manufacturing defect during installation, they will document it, and a replacement or refund is initiated.
- Customer self-assembly: Damage caused during self-assembly (stripped screws, cracked wood, misaligned parts) is not covered under warranty or return policy.

1.7 Large Item Returns and Reverse Pickup
- Furniture returns require a reverse pickup by a specialized logistics team.
- The item must be disassembled to the state it was delivered in. If HelperTom assembly was used, we will send a team to disassemble (charges may apply for change-of-mind returns).
- If the customer self-assembled, they must disassemble the item for pickup. The pickup team does not disassemble.
- Reverse pickup availability depends on the pin code. In non-serviceable areas, the customer may need to self-ship or coordinate with a local transporter (reimbursement up to INR 2000).

1.8 Refund Timelines
- Prepaid orders: Refund to source within 7-10 business days after quality inspection.
- Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer within 7-10 business days.
- For large furniture refunds, a deduction for return shipping (typically 10-15% of product value or actual freight cost, whichever is lower) may apply for change-of-mind returns. Defect returns have no deductions.

1.9 Non-Returnable Home Products
- Custom-made furniture and made-to-order items
- Assembled furniture (unless defective)
- Mattresses without a sleep trial program, once the packaging is opened
- Pillows, cushions, and bedding (once packaging opened, for hygiene)
- Rugs and carpets (once unrolled and used)
- Artificial plants and flowers with removed tags
- Candles that have been burned
- Wall art, prints, and posters once the protective wrap is removed
- Clocks with activated mechanisms or set time
- Personalized or monogrammed home decor items

SECTION 2: WARRANTY POLICY
----------------------------------------------

2.1 Furniture Warranty
- Solid Wood Furniture: 12 to 36 months against manufacturing defects (termite, borer, joint separation, structural failure).
- Engineered Wood (MDF, Particle Board, Plywood) Furniture: 6 to 12 months against manufacturing defects.
- Metal Furniture: 12 months against welding defects and rust (except surface rust from humidity).
- Upholstered Furniture: 12 months on frame and springs. Fabric and leather are not covered after the return window.
- Mechanisms (recliners, sofa-cum-beds, extendable tables): 12 months on mechanical parts.
- Glass: Not covered after delivery acceptance.

2.2 Mattress Warranty
- 5 to 10 years against manufacturing defects (sagging beyond specified limits, spring failure, foam splitting).
- Warranty specifics vary by brand. Check the warranty card for details.
- Warranty typically covers sagging greater than 1-1.5 inches for foam mattresses.
- Stains, tears, and improper foundation/base usage void the warranty.

2.3 Warranty Exclusions (General)
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from misuse, abuse, or accidents
- Improper cleaning or use of harsh chemicals
- Exposure to extreme temperatures, humidity, or direct sunlight
- Commercial use of residential-grade furniture
- Natural characteristics of materials (wood grain, leather marks)
- Customer modifications or unauthorized repairs

2.4 Warranty Claim Process
- Contact HelperTom support with purchase invoice and photographs of the defect.
- For some brands, an inspection visit is arranged.
- Approved claims result in repair, replacement part, or full replacement depending on the defect severity.

SECTION 3: CANCELLATION POLICY
----------------------------------------------

3.1 Cancellation Window
- Standard furniture and home decor: Cancellation within 2 hours or before dispatch.
- Made-to-order furniture: Cancellation within 24 hours and only if production has not started.
- Large orders (B2B, bulk): Custom cancellation terms as per order agreement.

3.2 Cancellation Charges
- No cancellation charges within the allowed window.
- Post-window cancellations for made-to-order items may incur a 20-30% restocking/production cost deduction.

SECTION 4: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
----------------------------------------------

4.1 Delivery Timelines
- In-stock Furniture: 7-15 business days (metro), 10-20 business days (non-metro).
- Made-to-order Furniture: 3-6 weeks (timeline specified on product page).
- Home Decor: 3-7 business days.

4.2 Shipping Charges
- Free shipping on furniture orders above INR 4,999.
- Below INR 4,999: Shipping charge based on weight, volume, and distance (calculated at checkout).
- Remote location delivery charges are additional and communicated before dispatch.

4.3 Delivery to Upper Floors
- Standard delivery includes ground floor placement.
- Upper floor delivery without elevator may incur additional charges (INR 200-500 per floor).
- This should be arranged and confirmed before delivery.

SECTION 5: HOME DECOR SPECIFICS
----------------------------------------------

5.1 Fragile Items (Mirrors, Glass, Ceramics)
- Must be inspected upon delivery with open box verification.
- Cracks or chips found after delivery must be reported within 24 hours with photos.
- Once accepted and the delivery team leaves, claims for glass breakage are difficult to process.

5.2 Wall Art and Frames
- Returnable within 7 days if protective wrapping is intact.
- Opened prints, posters, and canvases are non-returnable.
- Framed items with broken glass are eligible for replacement.

5.3 Lighting and Lamps
- Electrical functionality must be tested within 48 hours.
- Defective wiring, non-working sockets, or flickering is covered.
- Bulbs are consumable and not covered after the return window.
- Chandeliers and ceiling lights must be installed by a licensed electrician. Damage during installation is not covered.

5.4 Rugs and Carpets
- Returnable within 7 days if unrolled but unused with tags.
- Signs of use: foot traffic marks, flattening of pile, stains, pet hair, odors.
- Hand-knotted and hand-tufted rugs have natural variations in color and weave — these are not defects.
- Shedding is normal for new wool rugs and decreases over time.

5.5 Curtains and Blinds
- Custom-sized curtains and blinds are non-returnable.
- Ready-made curtains are returnable within 7 days if unopened or simply unfolded for inspection.
- Installed curtains and blinds are non-returnable.

SECTION 6: FURNITURE & HOME DECOR FAQ (30 QUESTIONS)
----------------------------------------------

Q1: I ordered a sofa, and the color looks completely different from the website. What can I do?
A: Color variations on screens are common. If the delivered sofa is fundamentally a different color (e.g., ordered beige, received dark brown), raise a dispute during Open Delivery or within 24 hours with photos. Our team will compare with the dispatch record and product listing. Mis-shipped colors are replaced. Minor shade variations are not covered.

Q2: My wooden dining table arrived with a small scratch on the surface. What are my options?
A: If identified during Open Delivery, note it on the POD and either refuse delivery or request a partial refund/repair. If discovered after delivery, report within 24 hours with photos. Depending on scratch severity, we may offer a touch-up kit, partial refund, or replacement.

Q3: Can I return a bookshelf I assembled myself but now realize doesn't fit in my room?
A: No. Once furniture is assembled, it cannot be returned for size or fit issues. Always measure your space carefully before ordering. Use our room planner tool where available.

Q4: I bought a mattress and it's too firm. Can I return it?
A: If the mattress brand offers a sleep trial (30/100 nights), you can return it under the trial terms. The mattress must be clean, stain-free, and without damage. If no sleep trial is offered and the packaging is opened, the mattress is non-returnable for comfort preference.

Q5: The delivery team damaged my wall while bringing in the sofa. Who is responsible?
A: HelperTom's logistics partners are trained to handle furniture carefully. If wall damage occurs, document it immediately with photos and note it on the POD. Report within 24 hours. We will coordinate with the logistics partner for repair or compensation.

Q6: How do I claim warranty on a sofa where the cushion has sagged after 6 months?
A: Check your warranty document for sagging thresholds. If within the warranty period, contact HelperTom support with photos and measurements showing the sag depth. An inspection may be arranged. If the sagging exceeds the warranted limit, foam replacement or cushion repair will be provided.

Q7: I received a coffee table with missing screws. Can I just get the hardware sent instead of returning the whole table?
A: Yes. For missing hardware, we can ship a hardware kit at no charge. Report with a list of missing parts (refer to the assembly manual). This is much faster than a full return. You do not need to return the entire table for missing screws.

Q8: Can I return an already assembled wardrobe if a door doesn't close properly?
A: Yes, this is a manufacturing defect. If assembly was done by our authorized team, they will document the issue, and we will arrange repair or replacement. If self-assembled, a technician will be sent to inspect. If the issue is due to assembly error, the service call will be chargeable.

Q9: My new bed frame makes creaking noises every time I move. Is this a defect?
A: Some creaking is normal in the initial settling period for wooden and metal beds. However, persistent loud creaking may indicate loose joints or hardware. Report within 7 days. Tightening the hardware often resolves it. If the issue persists, a technician visit will be arranged.

Q10: I ordered a rug online, and the colors are more muted than the product image. Is this returnable?
A: Minor color differences between digital images and physical products are expected and not considered defects. If the rug is a completely different color palette, you can return it within 7 days if unused with tags. Rugs that have been walked on are non-returnable.

Q11: What is the return policy for a bean bag that I find uncomfortable?
A: Bean bags can be returned within 7 days if unused and in original packaging. Once the bean bag filling has expanded and been used, it cannot be repackaged to its original state, making it non-returnable for comfort issues.

Q12: My wall clock stopped working after 3 days. What should I do?
A: Report within the 7-day window. A non-functional clock qualifies as a defect. Ensure you've tried a fresh battery. A replacement clock will be shipped, and the defective one picked up.

Q13: Can I return a chandelier if my electrician says it's incompatible with my ceiling?
A: Chandeliers are returnable within 7 days only if the box is unopened. Once opened and attempted for installation, it becomes non-returnable. Check the electrical specifications (voltage, mounting type, weight) before ordering.

Q14: I bought a plant pot, and it arrived cracked. It was a gift, and I opened it a week later. Is it still covered?
A: If you report the crack within 7 days of delivery (even if you opened the outer box later), it is covered. If the 7-day window has passed from the delivery date, it is not covered. We recommend opening and inspecting all fragile items immediately upon delivery, especially gifts ordered in advance.

Q15: The upholstery fabric on my new chair is pilling after one month. Is this covered?
A: Pilling depends on fabric type and usage. If pilling is excessive and occurs within the warranty period, contact support with photos. It will be evaluated against the fabric manufacturer's standards. Normal pilling from friction is not covered.

Q16: Can I cancel a custom-made sofa after it has been put into production?
A: Cancellation after production begins may incur a deduction of 20-30% of the product value to cover material and labor costs. Check the exact cancellation terms on your order or contact support to check the production status.

Q17: My dining chairs have uneven legs and wobble. The delivery was 5 days ago. What do I do?
A: Report within the 7-day window. Wobbling is a defect. Often, adjustable feet can resolve minor unevenness. If the issue is structural, a replacement chair or set will be provided.

Q18: What happens if my furniture delivery is delayed beyond the promised date?
A: Contact support for an updated ETA. Delays beyond 7 business days from the promised delivery window may make you eligible for a goodwill gesture (store credit or discount on future purchase). You can also cancel the order for a full refund if the delay is unacceptable.

Q19: I received a bookshelf made of particle board when I ordered solid wood. What can I do?
A: This is material misrepresentation. Document with photos and report immediately. This is a serious issue, and upon verification, you will receive a full refund, and the product will be picked up at no cost. Seller may face delisting.

Q20: Can I return a mattress after sleeping on it for a week under the sleep trial?
A: If the brand offers a sleep trial (clearly stated on the product page), yes. The mattress will be picked up under the trial terms. Ensure the mattress is clean, stain-free, and protected. Some brands require a minimum trial period (e.g., 15-30 days) before initiating return to allow for body adjustment.

Q21: I bought artificial flowers, and they look very fake compared to the image. Can I return them?
A: Artificial flowers can be returned within 7 days if the tags are intact and they are in original packaging. Aesthetic disappointment is an accepted return reason if the product is unused and repackaged.

Q22: The glass top of my center table shattered during delivery. The delivery team just left. What do I do?
A: This should have been caught during Open Delivery inspection. If you signed the POD without noting it, immediately photograph the damage and contact support within 2 hours of delivery. The tight timeline and photographic evidence are critical for processing a replacement or refund.

Q23: Can I return a floor lamp if the bulb flickers?
A: First, try a different bulb to ensure the issue is with the lamp socket and not the bulb. If the socket is faulty, report within 7 days for a replacement. Bulb issues alone are resolved with a replacement bulb, not a lamp return.

Q24: I want to exchange my sofa for a different color. Is that possible?
A: Exchanges for a different color are generally not supported once delivered. You would need to return the original sofa (if unassembled and within the return window, with return shipping charges) and place a new order for the preferred color.

Q25: What is the return policy for children's furniture like bunk beds and study tables?
A: Children's furniture follows the standard furniture return policy (7-10 days). Safety is critical. Any structural defect, sharp edges, or instability in children's furniture should be reported immediately. These claims are prioritized.

Q26: My new curtains have uneven stitching and crooked hems. Is this covered?
A: Yes, uneven stitching and crooked hems are manufacturing defects. Report within 7 days with photos. Ready-made curtains will be replaced. Custom-made curtains with such defects will be remade.

Q27: Can I return a bean bag cover if I don't like the fabric texture?
A: Yes, if the cover is unused, unzipped from the filling, and returned with original packaging within 7 days. If the filling has been inserted, it becomes difficult to return just the cover.

Q28: I assembled a shoe rack, and a wooden panel cracked during assembly. What should I do?
A: If the panel cracked under normal assembly pressure (following the manual), it is a material defect. Photograph and report within 7 days. If excessive force or wrong assembly caused the crack, it is not covered. We strongly recommend using professional assembly for particle board furniture.

Q29: I received a home decor showpiece, and the paint is chipped. The box was undamaged. Is this a defect?
A: Yes. If the outer box is undamaged but the product inside has chipped paint, it is a manufacturing defect that occurred before packaging. Report within 48 hours with photos for a replacement or refund.

Q30: What happens if I'm not home to receive my furniture delivery?
A: The delivery team will contact you to schedule a time slot. If you cannot be present, you can designate a representative (family member, neighbor, facility manager) to inspect and sign the POD on your behalf. Provide them with your order details and inspection instructions. Failed deliveries after multiple attempts may incur re-delivery charges.
